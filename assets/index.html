Urine Incontinence

Urinary incontinence is the accidental loss of urine. Stress incontinence, Urge incontinence, Mixed incontinence, Overflow incontinence, Functional incontinence, Reflex incontinence are types of incontinence.

Uterine Fibroid Surgery

Treatment for uterine fibroids may include medication, non-invasive techniques, or surgical options to help reduce symptoms and control fibroid growth within the uterus.

Organ Prolapse Surgery

When the supportive tissues surrounding a part of the body become weak or stretched, it can lead to a bulge, causing the organ to shift downward.

Azoospermia Treatment

Treatment for azoospermia focuses on resolving the lack of sperm in semen through approaches such as hormonal therapy, surgical interventions, or assisted reproductive methods.

Adenomyosis

Adenomyosis is a condition in which the tissue that normally lines the uterus starts to grow within the muscular walls of the uterus.

Endometriosis Treatment

Treatment for endometriosis may involve a combination of medication and surgery to alleviate pain, decrease inflammation, and enhance fertility.

Vaginal Agenesis

Vaginal agenesis, also known as Mayer-Rokitansky-Küster-Hauser (MRKH) syndrome, is a congenital condition where the vagina does not fully develop or is completely missing.

Gynecologic Endocrine Disorders

These are common clinical conditions that can give rise to a variety of related health issues. Women often experience challenges such as menstrual irregularities, dysmenorrhea, and breast swelling, which can significantly impact their daily lives.

Gynecologic Cancer

Gynecological cancers are types of cancer that develop in the female reproductive organs. These cancers include cervical, ovarian, uterine, vaginal, and vulvar cancers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and how we can assist you.

What services do you offer?

We provide a range of specialized services including maternity care, IVF treatments, advanced 3D laparoscopy, and more. Our goal is to ensure you receive the best care tailored to your needs.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by visiting our website and filling out the online form, or by calling our office directly. Our team will assist you in scheduling a convenient time for your visit.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring any relevant medical records, identification, and insurance information. This will help us provide you with the best care possible and ensure a smooth visit.

Do you offer insurance coverage?

We accept a variety of insurance plans. Please contact our office to confirm if your specific plan is accepted and to discuss any coverage details.

What is the process for IVF treatment?

Our IVF treatment process involves several steps including consultation, hormone therapy, egg retrieval, fertilization, and embryo transfer. Our team will guide you through each step to ensure the best possible outcome.

How can I contact you for further questions?

You can reach us by phone, email, or through our website's contact form. Our friendly staff is always available to answer any questions you may have.
